Please Enable Cookies
 

  To enjoy the most convenient and secure user experience at Vistaprint, your browser should be set to enable cookies.  

Cookies let us identify you as a registered user of our site, retrieve your account history and data, and tailor the site to your specific needs and preferences.

While the cookie remembers your user ID, it does not contain confidential information, such as your password and billing information. Please also be assured that these cookies are accessible only by Vistaprint. We do not distribute this information to other companies. For more details, please read our privacy policy.

After you have enabled cookies in your browser, please return to our site.

Instructions for enabling cookies:

  • In Internet Explorer® (including AOL)
  • In Firefox®
  • In Safari® (Mac)
How to enable cookies in Internet Explorer (including AOL)
Back To Top
  • Click on Tools on the main menu.
  • Select Internet Options.
  • Click on the Privacy tab.
  • Click on the Advanced button.
  • Check the checkbox labeled Override automatic cookie handling.
  • Check the checkbox labeled Always allow session cookies.
  • Click on the OK button.
  • Click on the OK button.

After you have enabled cookies in your browser, please return to our site.

How to enable cookies in Firefox
Back To Top
  • Click on Tools on the main menu.
  • Select the Privacy icon.
  • Click on the Cookies tab.
  • Check the checkbox labeled Allow sites to set cookies.
  • Make sure the checkbox labeled Unless I have removed cookies set by the site is unchecked.
  • Click on the OK button.

After you have enabled cookies in your browser, please return to our site.

How to enable cookies in Safari (Mac)
Back To Top
  • Open Safari.
  • From the Safari menu, click Preferences.
  • From the Security tab, under Accept Cookies, click to select Always.
  • Click Close.

After you have enabled cookies in your browser, please return to our site.
